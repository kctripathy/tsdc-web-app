About

TSD College is a 'NAAC' accredidated Grade 'B' college, established in the year 1981, affiliated to CHSE Odisha and Berhampur University.

It is a premier college located in Bijaya Dhanurjay Pur (Sasan), Ganjam Odisha and known for its excellence educational standards, various educational programs, grand faculty and modern infrastructure.

Vision

The mission is to promote discipline, healthy outlook and positive attitudes to contribute for national integration

To promote quality academic in the locality with the assistance of the NAAC, UGC and Govt. of Odisha.
