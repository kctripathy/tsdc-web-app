Library being the heart of an institution is a power house of knowledge and support the information needs of all its users.

To enrich the library, every year addition of valuable books on varied topics of study are suggested and recommended by different department. Reprography and Internet facilities are made available to the users.

::

1. College Library shall remain open during the college hours on working days.

2. Books will be used / received from 10:30Am. To 1:30Pm. And rest period is set apart for library official work.

3. Books will be issued to the students on production of the library card along with college Identity card.

4. No book is to be taken out before the student puts his/her signature on the issue register.

5. Students must have to examine the condition of the book issued and bring to the notice of the Librarian if any damage is found before taking it.

6. Books should be returned with in two weeks, otherwise time as fixed from time to time by the college authority shall be changed. If the students does not returned the book(s) action will be taken as deemed fit.

7. If the date of return falls on a holiday, it shall be accepted on the next working day or any other notified with out time.

8. Books will be issued as per the dates notified for that purpose.

9. All books should be returned before the summer vacation/filling up forms for university/CHSE examination, which ever is earlier.

10. Except the members of the staff, no one else is allowed to enter inside the library.

11. No one is allowed to take reference books, maps, journals course of studies, question papers and Granthabali neither to take out side the library shall be issued to any one.

12. Books lost or damaged must be replaced by the borrower or else there times of the price of the book shall be changed from the borrower.
